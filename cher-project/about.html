Chismis: Gossip in Tagalog.

Let's spill the chismis about Chissmiss Cherry! She's a DJ specializing in Top 100, House, Hip Hop and Throwbacks.

In 2020, she joined UForChange’s DJ arts program. Since then, she has grown and graduated into playing live gigs.

For tasty tunes guaranteed, book Chissmiss Cherry for birthdays, weddings, club or corporate events.
